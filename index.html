About Me

I'm a third-year Software Engineering student at UOL with a deep passion for building software that solves real problems. My journey started with curiosity about how websites work and quickly evolved into a love for clean code, thoughtful UI design, and scalable backend systems.

Whether I'm debugging a stubborn algorithm at midnight or collaborating on a team project, I bring the same energy: focused, detail-oriented, and genuinely excited about the process. I believe great software is as much about empathy for the user as it is about technical excellence.
